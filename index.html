MIM and Problem Management

AI Co-Pilot for Bridge Calls

Description:

During major incidents, Incident Managers are required to manage the bridge, communicate with stakeholders, and take notes at the same time. This leads to missed updates, unclear action items, and poor post-call documentation. 

AI Solution:

The AI Co-Pilot joins the bridge call as a virtual assistant. It listens to the conversation, transcribes everything in real-time, identifies speakers, capture all investigation chronology, highlights key decisions, captures action items with timestamps and owners, and finally sends a structured summary to Major Incident manager to cross check and re verify after the call ends. 

Benefits:

Reduces manual effort, improves the quality of documentation, ensures no critical point is missed, and accelerates RCA and learning post-incident. 

Preferred Tools: N/A

Auto-Stakeholder Tagging & Bridge Creation

Description:

When a major incident is declared, the on-call engineer often struggle  identifying and inviting the right stakeholders. Incorrect or delayed tagging causes confusion and resolution delays. 

AI Solution:

The AI system reads the incident details (from monitoring or ITSM tools), matches it against historical stakeholder mappings, and automatically creates a bridge (Teams/Zoom), names it properly, and after confirmation from MIM it will send the invite

Benefits:

Reduces bridge setup time, ensures accurate stakeholder involvement, and cuts down Mean Time to Resolution (MTTR)

RCA Suggestion Post-Incident

Description:

Root Cause Analysis is often repetitive and delayed because it requires manual correlation of incident data, logs, and history. 

AI Solution:

After incident resolution, the AI system analyzes related data, compares it with past incidents, and automatically suggests probable root causes, corrective actions, and related KB articles or Known Error Records (KERs).

Benefits:

Speeds up RCA, improves accuracy, and reduces repeated effort by leveraging historical learning.

Auto-Proactive Problem Creation (Problem Management) 

Description:

Repeated low-severity incidents often go unnoticed as related, causing delays in identifying underlying problems.

AI Solution:

The AI observes patterns across incidents — if multiple similar ones occur over a few days/weeks/months, it clusters them and auto-creates mail to problem management team. It will also suggest potential root causes and suggest the right resolver team to Problem Managers. 

Benefits:

Prevents future major incidents by catching root causes early, reducing ticket noise and service disruption.
